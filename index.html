Biggest Mobile Companies and Their Smartphones

Samsung: Leading with about 20-23% market share, Samsung offers a wide range of smartphones including flagship Galaxy S and foldable Galaxy Z series. Known for innovation like AI-powered cameras and premium displays, it appeals to both premium and mid-range segments.

Apple: Holding around 18-28% market share, Apple is famous for its premium iPhones and expanding aggressively in emerging markets. It combines powerful hardware with smooth iOS experience.

Xiaomi: Known for budget-friendly smartphones with flagship features, Xiaomi targets price-sensitive consumers globally and is expanding its premium lineup.

Oppo and Vivo: Both brands emphasize camera innovation and stylish designs, holding around 5-8% market share each, and gaining strong global followings.

Other notable brands include Realme, Honor, Transsion, Huawei, and Motorola, each contributing to a diverse and competitive smartphone market.
